What is CitoEngine?

Configuring monitoring systems to alert properly is an art. It's a fine art of configuring thresholds when your monitoring parameters vary widely or when the monitoring tools lack capability to monitor dynamic workloads. It also takes discipline in working with monitoring systems during release process or outages. Not all monitoring systems are configured or maintained properly. In the ned you have alerts and lots and lots of it. CitoEngine allows you to manage large volume of alerts and trigger action to notify or act on the alert by executing a script (a plugin).
